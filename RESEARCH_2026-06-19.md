# Research log — Futures/DEX income search + options↔futures linkage — 2026-06-19

Goal: find a path to ~$1–2/day, test deposit $400, scalable to $1000→$5000, low/controllable risk.
Method throughout: honest backtest with train/holdout split, realistic fees/slippage/funding,
per-side checks to catch directional leak. Two repos: ~/Desktop/Fun (HL DEX) and ~/Desktop/options
(ETH options + this investigation). All harnesses below also copied to VPS3 /root/opt-app.

## Hard arithmetic (the framing)
$1–2/day on $400 = 0.25–0.5%/day = **150–500% APR**. That is NOT a modest bar — it is an
extraordinary low-risk return nobody delivers reliably. AI doesn't repeal risk↔return; it makes
markets MORE efficient. Low-risk yield on $400 ≈ $0.09–0.16/day. $1–2/day low-risk needs ~$5k.
So the target is mis-scaled to the capital, not a failure of tooling.

## Strategies tested & VERDICTS

### Linear / delta perp strategies — ALL REJECTED (same disease: disguised direction, flips OOS)
1. **Spot-perp basis arb (Fun)** — closed. Live L2 (basis_logger) confirmed no executable edge; spread > gap always.
2. **Concentrated LP, unhedged & HEDGED (Fun, lp_backtest.py)** — rejected. Delta-neutral short-perp hedge
   strips direction but exposes that fees < IL/LVR + costs at realistic ETH/USDC fee APR (~50–100%);
   break-even needs ~150–200% gross APR (ETH/USDC doesn't sustain). Funding +3.8% is a small tailwind.
   Validated hedge math vs AMM identity dV=∫x·dp. Classic LVR result; scaling doesn't fix a negative APR.
3. **Perp trend-follow (earlier)** — rejected, holdout −66%.
4. **H1 positioning-crowding mean-reversion (perp_positioning_backtest.py)** — rejected. Long side loses
   every horizon, only short wins in a downtrend = directional leak. Data is one downtrend regime.
5. **H2 IV-cone VRP mean-reversion (perp_iv_cone_backtest.py)** — rejected. Used REAL DVOL implied vol.
   TRAIN negative in every config; VRP gate never rescues. VRP is a vega edge — can't carry it on a linear future.
6. **A ETH/BTC market-neutral pairs (perp_eth_btc_pairs_backtest.py)** — rejected. Picked because
   delta-neutral should kill the leak; but the ETH/BTC ratio TRENDS regime-by-regime, doesn't mean-revert
   → leak just moved from market-direction to ratio-direction. TRAIN negative every config.

### Options↔futures complement (user's idea) — futures overlay REJECTED, but yielded the real win
- **option_futures_complement.py**: long perp opposite the short-call delta IS +EV on the SL subset
  (+$3–4k in-sample) — thesis correct — but as a blanket/triggered overlay always cuts total & barely
  helps maxDD (bleeds on the 77% winners). 8-core parallel gen + cache.
- **sl_predictor.py / sl_gate_oos.py**: SL is predictable in-sample & causally (thin/neg VRP → 39% SL;
  rich VRP → 15%; low IV → 2% SL; mid-high IV → 41%). But OOS the gate gives NO profit edge: B-hedge
  loses OOS, A-skip ≈ flat profit (cuts SL & ~30% maxDD only at −32% profit). Independently re-derives
  the project's iv_richness_filter but on REAL IV.

### Options strategy strengthening on REAL implied vol (Deribit DVOL) — the GENUINE WIN
- Fetched **real ETH IV: Deribit DVOL hourly, 16 months → data/eth_dvol_1h.json** (+ btc_funding.json).
- **realiv_rerun.py**: re-priced the validated short-CALL strategy on real IV (monkeypatch, no edits to
  the validated engine). Edge is ROBUST, ~+4.5%/trade, train≈holdout — NOT a σ=0.6 artifact.
- **realiv_improve.py**: **WIN — trade CALLS in `transition` regime only (drop `range`)**: holdout
  +4.49→+5.11%/trade, SL 17.2→15.0%, only −96/523 trades. Robust train≈holdout. → set call-side
  regime_filter=["transition"]. (Overfit caught & rejected: thin-VRP entry gate looked best on train
  (+7.59%) and worst on holdout (+0.91%).)
- **realiv_mixed.py**: MIXED book (range-puts + transition-calls) on real IV — strong OOS per-trade edge
  both sides. NOTE: its raw "$5/day @ $400" is an ARTIFACT (assumes unlimited concurrent capital;
  $400 margin can't run 736 overlapping 24–96h positions). Real $/day is margin/concurrency-bound.
- **Round 4 (iv_mixed_deposit.py / deposit_sim.py)**: honest margin-constrained $/day (MAX_OPEN=4, 15%
  margin, fees, dyn-size, circuit-breaker, compounding). RESULT @ $400, full year:
  - MIXED-24 (calls@24h + puts@96h) = **$400→$612.57, +53.1% APR, maxDD 24%, ~$0.60/day**; holdout
    +12.75%/trade, Sharpe 0.17 (robust). Baseline 7d = +5.9% only (holdout +0.02%/trade ≈ flat).
    MIXED-48+FLOOR showed +293% but on 99 trades, maxDD 36% = high-variance/overfit, NOT banked.
  - **SMOKING GUN for the scaling thesis:** strategy GENERATES 4.26 signals/day but $400 account TAKES
    only 0.94/day (332/1509); 78% blocked (cap=759, margin=233). The edge is real; $400 is too small
    to harvest it. $1–2/day is reachable by SCALING capital (unblocks signals) on the SAME validated edge.
